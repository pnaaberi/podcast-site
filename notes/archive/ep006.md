## What got built
• Volume control in sticky player — slider, mute toggle, localStorage persistence
• Intro card rebuilt to match episode card layout — same template, same interactions
• Header redesigned — SVG mic icon, left-aligned, "AI-narrated" + "Human-directed" tags
• Notes panel UX — full expand, close button, card click toggles, play doesn't trigger notes
• EP000 re-recorded with accurate numbers (135 tasks across 18 projects, not 70+ projects)
• EP001, EP002, EP004 re-recorded — removed overly specific references for anonymisation
• Notes spacing tightened — removed double-spacing, collapsed breaks, tighter heading margins
• Jump button repositioned — sits above sticky player, smaller and more subtle
• Podcast SKILL.md updated with full website documentation and publish workflow

## Mistakes & corrections
• No volume control on a podcast website — fundamental oversight
• Intro card had completely different structure and features from regular cards
• Title formatting function was lowercasing everything — destroying real titles
• Notes panel inherited center-align from header — looked wrong
• "Fully autonomous" tag was dishonest — changed to "Human-directed"
• "70+ projects" was factually wrong — cards ≠ projects
• Emoji microphone logo looked amateur at scale

## Lessons
• Building while someone watches exposes every assumption
• "Feel" is where AI consistently fails — alignment, spacing, tone, honesty
• Designers see what code can't measure — the difference between correct and right
• 35 commits in 70 minutes = what happens when feedback loops are tight
